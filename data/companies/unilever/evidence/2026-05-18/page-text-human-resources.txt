Unlocking our potential.

Our Teams

We are powered by three functions working together as 'One HR' and each playing a pivotal role in shaping an exceptional employee experience and driving business success.

People Experience

The heartbeat of HR. Every day there are thousands of interactions between People Experience and our people themselves where process, technology and, most importantly, the human touch come together to make every moment in the employee lifecycle at Unilever matter.

Expertise

We have centres of excellence that design, develop and deliver our global best practices and strategic HR initiatives. This includes teams such as Talent; Equity, Diversity and Inclusion; Reward; Pensions and Benefits; Learning; Organisation Development and People Analytics; Employer Brand; and Global Health and Wellbeing to name just a few.
